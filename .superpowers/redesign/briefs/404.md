## 5. Edge cases / decisions left to the implementer

- **Display scale h1** — `--text-display` is 5rem / 80px. On a phone (375px wide), this is 21% of viewport width — readable. **Implementer may add `clamp(3.6rem, 8vw, 5rem)` for fluid scaling**, but the existing fixed-size h1 is acceptable.
- **No photos on this page** — display-scale h1 is the "earnest" element; no photos needed.
- **No CuratedMark, no CuratedPicks, no NewsletterBand** on this page.
- **noindex meta tag** — keep.
- **The page is rendered by Astro at build time** for any non-matched route (`src/pages/404.astro`). Static output. No client-side JS needed.
