A small operations dashboard for one-room cafés. Daily sales, supplier orders, a low-stock list, and a notes field for the day's weird thing. The whole product is one route per concern, written as MDX with a sprinkle of client-side state. The brief was: make it feel like a paper ledger, not a SaaS dashboard. The first version fit on a single page.
