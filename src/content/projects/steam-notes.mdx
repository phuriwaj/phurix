title: "steam notes"
desc: "A small editor for the things you read while your coffee cools. End-to-end encrypted drafts."
tags: ["TypeScript", "IndexedDB", "WebCrypto"]
year: 2024
---

A scratchpad for short, disposable notes — the kind you write while waiting for a cortado, then never look at again. Drafts live in IndexedDB and sync to a remote blob store when the user opts in. The local-only default is the point: the product is for thoughts that don't need to live forever. Encryption is opt-in but the architecture is set up to support it from day one.
